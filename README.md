## 🛒 Amazon Product Analysis — SQL Case Study
A SQL-based analysis of an Amazon product dataset, exploring pricing, discounts, ratings, and customer reviews using MySQL queries.

## 🔍 Queries Solved

1. Listed all products with a discounted price below ₹500
2. Found products with a discount percentage of 50% or more
3. Retrieved all products whose name contains the word "Cable"
4. Calculated the difference between average actual price and average discounted price per product
5. Queried products whose reviews mention "fast charging"
6. Identified products with a discount percentage between 20% and 40%
7. Found products priced above ₹1,000 with a rating of 4 stars or above
8. Listed products where the discounted price ends with a 9
9. Filtered reviews containing negative keywords like "worst", "waste", "poor", or "not good"
10. Listed all products belonging to categories that include "Accessories"


## 🛠️ Tools Used
MySQL — SELECT, WHERE, LIKE, BETWEEN, HAVING, GROUP BY, AVG, ROUND
